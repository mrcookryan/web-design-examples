Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 48–72 hours in advance for standard trips. For weddings, proms, or large group events, book 2–4 weeks ahead to guarantee your preferred vehicle.

Do you track flight arrivals for airport pickups?

Yes! We monitor your flight in real time and adjust pickup time automatically if your flight is delayed — at no extra charge. Just include your flight number in the booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24+ hours before pickup receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a partial charge. Contact us to discuss your situation.

Are gratuity and fuel included in pricing?

Our quotes are all-inclusive — gratuity and fuel are always included. You'll never see hidden charges added at checkout. What we quote is what you pay.

Do you offer corporate accounts?

Absolutely. We offer monthly invoicing, dedicated account managers, and volume discounts for businesses with ongoing transportation needs. Contact us to set up an account.

Are your vehicles accessible?

We're committed to serving all passengers. Please mention any accessibility requirements in your booking request and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.
